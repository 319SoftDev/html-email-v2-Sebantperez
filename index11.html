SECURITY UPDATES

Random Scanning for Weapons

The NYC Department of Education conducts random scans for weapons. Please be aware of the following categories:

 

Category I Weapons: Firearms, stun guns, air guns, switchblades, explosives

Category II Weapons: Pepper spray, imitation weapons, sharp objects

 

Possession of these items can result in suspension, so please stay informed and safe.

 

For any questions or concerns, contact us at security@hsfi.edu.
