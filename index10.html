Security Updates

Random Scanning for Weapons

The NYC Department of Education conducts random scanning for weapons. Make sure to follow the guidelines:

  • Category I Weapons

    • Firearms, stun guns, air guns, switchblades
    • Explosives

  • Category II Weapons

    • Pepper spray, imitation weapons, sharp objects

Possession of these items will lead to suspension, so be informed and stay safe.

If you have questions or concerns, please reach out to us at security@hsfi.edu.
